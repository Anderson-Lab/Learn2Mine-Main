Welcome to Learn2Mine's Galaxy Page.
Here, you'll be given all of the tools you need to learn the art of data science, through mining and manipulation. To get started, check out the "Learn2Mine Toolset" tab to your left, and pick the lesson you'd like to try for! Once you get your results, try grading them by using the "Submit to Learn2Mine" tool.













Learn2Mine is a project created by students Jake Dierksheide and Clayton Turner of the College of Charleston, under the guidance of Dr. Paul Anderson.




Learn2Mine is part of a series of projects by the Bioinformatics Research Group (BiRG), started by Dr. Paul Anderson. For more information, visit The BiRG Website
