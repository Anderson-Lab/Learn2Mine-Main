Welcome to Learn2Mine!

Learn2Mine hopes to be an effective way for people of all backgrounds and domains specialties to be able learn and practice the growing discipline of data science. You can select from our wide range of lessons by going viewing your profile. If it's your first time here we recommend trying the Basic R Lesson, the Empirical Naive Bayes Lesson, or the Leave-one-out Cross-validation Lesson. These lessons will allow you to start by getting your feet wet in the world of data science practice.
